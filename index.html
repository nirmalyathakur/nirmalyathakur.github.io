Want A Sneak Peek? — Here I Am!

Hey! Do you know we originated from the Central Plains of North America about 40 million years ago? Yeah! this is true and as of now we have a world wide family of around 158,000!

Early Life

Most of our ancestors had migrated from North America to South America about three million years ago during the Great American Interchange.

During the Ice Age

By the end of the last ice age (10,000 to 12,000 years ago), our ancestors living in North America were no more. I so wish they should have come along with the rest to South America.

Our Origin

We are basically native to the high puna of the South American Andes. Peru and Bolivia form the heart of this region with portions of Argentina, Chile, Colombia, and Ecuador forming the periphery.

Our Family

We belong to the family of "Camelidae." I am not sure but I have heard that we have originated from a North American ancestor who is also the supposed predecessor of the African and Asian camels.
